About Me

Hi, my name is Eleanor and I am currently studying at iO Academy, training to be a web developer. I can't wait to learn more coding languages and get stuck into new projects!

What I can do

Web Development

When I first started the course, I expected front-end would appeal to me most, but I have really thrived on the challenge of learning PHP and getting to grips with databases.

Ability To Learn

Over the course of four months, I have learnt over 7 different coding languages. I have hugely enjoyed the process and cannot wait to learn more!

Problem Solving

After struggling through a task for hours, it is the most rewarding feeling to crack it. Once I get started, it's hard to stop until the problem is solved!

Productivity

I thought the A Levels I recently completed were challenging but the iO course was demanding on a whole new level! It has demanded meticulous planning, both individually and with my dev team, and I am amazed by how much my productivity has improved.

Communication

For a development project to be successful, I believe communication with the client is just as important as the quality of the code itself. I have discovered I'm a natural communicator and look forward to leading interaction with the client.

Team Work

Team work and pair programming have been a crucial element of the course and undoubtedly one of my favourite aspects. I enjoy the buzz of a team-based challenge and am now a Certified Scrum Master.
